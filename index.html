Professional Summary

Dr. Mohnish Dighe is a highly experienced and compassionate Neurosurgeon with over 15 years of post-graduate surgical experience and more than 10 years of super-specialization in Neurosurgery. He completed his MBBS from MGM Medical College, Mumbai, followed by DNB in General Surgery from Lokmanya Hospital, Pune, and DNB in Neurosurgery from Bokaro General Hospital, Bokaro Steel City. He further served as Assistant Professor in Neurosurgery at Nair Hospital, Mumbai. His areas of expertise include trauma surgeries of the brain and spine, and tumor surgeries. Known for surgical precision, ethical approach, and patient-friendly care.
